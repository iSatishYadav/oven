Indian Roti Basket/Ghee Mode

  • Press STOP/Cancel.

  • Press Indian Roti Basket/Ghee until a desired code (1, z) appears in the display.

    • 1: Indian Roti Basket

    • z: Ghee

  • Select a desired menu code by using More/Less, and press START/Select.

  • Select a desired weight by using More/Less, and press START/Select.

    • During cooking you can turn the dial to adjust the cooking time.

    • When the beep, turn food over and press START/Select to resume the cook cycle.
