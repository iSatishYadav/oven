Quick Start Feature

This feature allows you to set 30-second intervals of High power cooking with each press of the START/Select button.

Cooking for 2 Minutes on High Power

  • Press STOP/Cancel.

  • Press START/Select four times.

    • The oven starts cooking and the time counts down in the display.

Note

  • The oven will immediately start cooking for 30 seconds on high power.

  • Press START/Select repeatedly to extend the cook time by 30 seconds every time the button is pressed.

  • The cook time can be extended to 99 minutes and 59 seconds.
